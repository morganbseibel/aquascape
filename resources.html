For more information about this website's creator:

      Morgan Seibel learned about aquascaping for the first time three years ago. Ever since then, she has done extensive research on the topic and has contributed to the creation of multiple aquascapes. She has gotten the majority of her information for this website from the above resources. She has also gathered information from people who aquascape and learned a lot of information about aquascaping through first-hand experiences. She has been involved in the entire aquascaping process of multiple tanks, from initial planting to watching a fully established tank flourish with fish. Her research, relationships with other aquascapers and personal experiences aquascaping have helped her to create this accurate, informational and interesting website about aquascaping.
